## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite relies on clinician expertise to maintain accurate, relevant, and evidence-based content. There are several ways to contribute to the platform's ongoing improvement.

## Submitting Feedback

### Via Email

Send detailed feedback to [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with:

- The specific page or protocol you are referencing
- A clear description of the issue or suggestion
- Supporting references or evidence (if applicable)

### Via Community Forum

Post in the **Platform Feedback** topic area on the [community forum](https://community.anesthesiasmartsite.com) for broader discussion before formal submission.

## Reporting Clinical Accuracy Issues

If you identify potentially inaccurate clinical information (incorrect dosing, outdated guidelines, unsupported claims):

1. Note the specific page and content in question
2. Email [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with the subject line "Clinical Accuracy Report"
3. Include the correct information and supporting references
4. The clinical content team will review and respond within 48 hours

<Callout kind="info">
  Clinical accuracy reports are given priority in the review queue. Your contribution directly supports patient safety.
</Callout>
