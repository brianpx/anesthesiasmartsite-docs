## Overview

The Anesthesia SmartSite community forum at [community.anesthesiasmartsite.com](https://community.anesthesiasmartsite.com) connects anesthesiologists to discuss clinical practices, share experiences with AI integration, and collaborate on evidence-based care.

## Joining the Forum

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Community" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [community.anesthesiasmartsite.com](https://community.anesthesiasmartsite.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Forum Account" icon="user-plus">
    Register using your Anesthesia SmartSite credentials or create a separate community account. Use your professional identity to build credible connections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Discussion Topics" icon="message-circle">
    Browse existing discussions by topic area and introduce yourself in the Welcome thread.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Discussion Areas

| Topic | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **AI in Practice** | Experiences applying AI tools and the VDD framework in clinical settings |
| **Clinical Protocols** | Discussion of evidence-based protocols, case-based learning, and protocol adaptations |
| **Education & CME** | Study groups, learning resources, and CME activity discussions |
| **Platform Feedback** | Suggestions for Anesthesia SmartSite content and feature improvements |
| **General Discussion** | Professional networking and open topics in anesthesiology |

## Community Guidelines

- Maintain professional conduct in all interactions
- Do not share patient-identifiable information (all case discussions must be fully de-identified)
- Support claims with evidence and citations when discussing clinical practices
- Report inaccurate clinical information to moderators
- Respect diverse institutional practices and clinical perspectives

<Callout kind="warning">
  Never post patient-identifiable information. All case discussions must be fully de-identified per HIPAA and institutional privacy policies.
</Callout>
