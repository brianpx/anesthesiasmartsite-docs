<Update label="2026-03-30" description="v2.0.0" tags={["feature", "improvement", "docs"]}>
  ## Documentation Overhaul

  Complete restructuring of the Anesthesia SmartSite documentation with 49 pages organized by clinical workflows.

  ## New Sections

  - **AI in Anesthesiology** — 6 pages covering VDD Framework, Beyond the Hype CME, AI Governance, Resource Guide, and Knowledge Quizzes
  - **Clinical Protocols** — 5 pages including Awake Spine Surgery, Patient Selection, Anesthetic Techniques, and Perioperative Management
  - **Clinical Education** — 4 pages covering CME Materials, Presentations, and Earning CME Credit
  - **Your Account** — 5 pages for Account Setup, Security & 2FA, Profile, Favorites, and Search
  - **Community** — Forum and Contributing guides

  ## Improvements

  - Expanded Help Center from 3 to 19 pages (FAQs, How-To Guides, Troubleshooting)
  - Replaced placeholder API reference with clinical endpoints, VDD documentation schema, and proper authentication docs
  - Restructured navigation into 6 workflow-oriented groups for better discoverability
  - Replaced pseudocode examples with clinical scenarios in AI documentation
  - Added clinical disclaimers to all protocol pages
</Update>
