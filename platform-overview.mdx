## What Is Anesthesia SmartSite?

Anesthesia SmartSite is a clinical education platform organized around three pillars: AI in anesthesiology, evidence-based protocols, and continuing medical education. Every resource is reviewed against current evidence and designed for direct application in perioperative practice.

<Callout kind="info">
  All clinical content is reviewed against published guidelines and current evidence. Protocols must be adapted to your institutional policies and individual patient factors.
</Callout>

## AI in Anesthesiology

The AI section provides a structured approach to integrating artificial intelligence into anesthesiology practice. The centerpiece is the **VDD Framework** (Verify, Decide, Document), a three-step methodology ensuring that every AI-assisted clinical decision is verified against evidence, weighed with clinical judgment, and documented for accountability.

Key resources include:
- **VDD Framework** — The core methodology for supervised AI use
- **Beyond the Hype CME** — Accredited presentation on responsible AI deployment
- **AI Governance Principles** — Institutional frameworks for ethical oversight, transparency, and continuous audit
- **Resource Guide** — Curated articles on clinical AI governance and implementation
- **Knowledge Quizzes** — Interactive assessments to test and reinforce AI supervision skills

## Clinical Protocols

Evidence-based protocols cover specialized procedures and general perioperative management. Current protocol areas include:

- **Awake Spine Surgery** — Complete procedural walkthrough from patient selection through post-operative care
- **Patient Selection Criteria** — ASA classification, psychological suitability, and BMI considerations
- **Anesthetic Techniques** — Monitored anesthesia care (MAC) with dexmedetomidine, sedation scoring, and local anesthesia protocols
- **Perioperative Management** — Induction checklists, maintenance parameters, and emergence criteria

<Callout kind="warning">
  Protocols must be adapted to your institutional policies, patient population, and individual clinical circumstances. This content does not replace clinical judgment.
</Callout>

## Clinical Education

The education section supports ongoing professional development through:

- **CME Materials** — Accredited educational content with learning objectives and assessments
- **Presentations** — Structured lectures including the "Beyond the Hype" AI presentation
- **Interactive Quizzes** — Knowledge assessments covering AI supervision, VDD methodology, and clinical protocols
- **CME Credit** — Step-by-step guidance for earning and reporting continuing education credits
