## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser such as Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- Internet connection for accessing the platform
</Callout>

These requirements ensure a smooth onboarding experience to Anesthesia SmartSite's evidence-based resources for anesthesiologists.

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the Anesthesia SmartSite sign-up page at `https://anesthesiasmartsite.com/signup`.

    Fill in your professional details:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Secure password (at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit and Verify" icon="mail">
    Click **Create Account**.

    Check your email inbox (and spam folder) for a verification link from Anesthesia SmartSite. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="user">
    Return to the sign-up page or dashboard prompt. Add optional details like your medical license number or specialty focus (e.g., neuroanesthesia) to personalize recommendations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use your professional email for verification. This helps access premium clinical education content faster.
</Callout>

## Log In to the Dashboard

Access your personalized dashboard with these login options.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email/Password" icon="mail">
    Go to `https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login`.

    Enter your email and password, then click **Sign In**.

    Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) when prompted for enhanced security.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Single Sign-On (SSO)" icon="shield">
    If your institution supports SSO (e.g., via Google Workspace or hospital SAML):

    Select **Login with SSO** on the login page.

    Choose your provider and authorize access. This method is recommended for clinical teams.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Access Your First Resource

Start with the **Beyond the Hype** presentation on supervised AI in anesthesiology.

1. From the dashboard, click **AI Resources**.
2. Select **Resource Guide** or **Knowledge Quiz**.
3. Review the VDD framework: Verify AI outputs, Decide on clinical action, Document your process.

This introductory content equips you with practical tools for safe AI deployment.

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Common Issues" default-open="false">
  **Forgot Password?**
  - Click **Forgot Password** on the login page and follow the reset link.

  **Verification Email Not Received?**
  - Check spam/junk. Contact support@anesthesiasmartsite.com if needed.

  **Browser Issues?**
  - Clear cache or try incognito mode.
</Expandable>
