## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite hosts structured educational presentations covering AI in anesthesiology and clinical topics. Presentations are accessible online through the platform dashboard and are designed for individual review or institutional use during grand rounds and departmental education.

## Available Presentations

### Beyond the Hype: Supervised AI in Anesthesiology

The primary educational presentation, developed in collaboration with IARS, covers responsible AI deployment in perioperative care.

- **Format:** Online structured presentation with interactive elements
- **Topics:** AI evidence assessment, VDD Framework, clinical scenarios, institutional governance
- **CME eligible:** Yes — see [Earning CME Credit](/education/earning-cme-credit) for details
- **Full details:** [Beyond the Hype CME](/ai/beyond-the-hype)

## Accessing Presentations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Education" icon="book-open">
    From your dashboard, select **Clinical Education** or navigate to `/#education`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Presentation" icon="monitor">
    Choose the presentation you want to review. Each listing shows the topic, duration, and CME eligibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete at Your Pace" icon="clock">
    Presentations support pause and resume. Your progress is saved automatically so you can return where you left off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  If a presentation is not loading or playing correctly, see [Video/Presentation Playback](/help-center/troubleshooting/playback-issues) for troubleshooting steps.
</Callout>

## Using Presentations for Institutional Education

Presentations can be used for departmental education purposes:

- **Grand rounds** — Present during scheduled department meetings with live discussion
- **Journal club** — Use presentation content as a starting point for evidence-based discussion
- **Onboarding** — Include in new staff orientation for AI governance awareness
- **Self-directed learning** — Individual clinicians complete presentations at their own pace
