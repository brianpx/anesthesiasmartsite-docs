## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite provides continuing medical education (CME) resources focused on evidence-based anesthesiology practice and AI integration in perioperative care. All educational content includes defined learning objectives, structured assessments, and pathways to earn CME credit.

## Learning Pathways

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AI in Anesthesiology" icon="cpu">
    Build competency in supervised AI use for clinical practice.

    1. Complete the [VDD Framework](/ai/vdd-framework) guide
    2. Review the [AI Governance Principles](/ai/governance)
    3. Take the [Beyond the Hype CME](/ai/beyond-the-hype) presentation
    4. Pass the post-presentation assessment
    5. Complete the [AI Supervision Quiz](/ai/quizzes) and [VDD Quiz](/ai/quizzes) for self-assessment
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Clinical Protocols" icon="clipboard-list">
    Develop expertise in specialized anesthesia procedures.

    1. Review the [Protocols Overview](/protocols/overview) to understand available protocols
    2. Study the [Patient Selection Criteria](/protocols/patient-selection)
    3. Review [Anesthetic Techniques](/protocols/anesthetic-techniques) for MAC procedures
    4. Complete the [Awake Spine Surgery](/protocols/awake-spine-surgery) protocol
    5. Review [Perioperative Management](/protocols/perioperative-management) checklists
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
