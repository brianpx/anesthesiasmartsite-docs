## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite offers accredited CME materials focused on AI integration in anesthesiology and evidence-based perioperative care. Each course includes defined learning objectives, structured content, and a post-completion assessment.

## Available CME Courses

### Beyond the Hype: Supervised AI in Anesthesiology

The flagship CME course examining responsible AI deployment in perioperative care.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Format** | Online presentation with interactive elements |
| **Duration** | Approximately 1 hour |
| **Credit** | AMA PRA Category 1 Credit(s) |
| **Assessment** | Post-presentation quiz (minimum passing score required) |
| **Access** | [Beyond the Hype CME](/ai/beyond-the-hype) |

**Learning objectives:**
- Distinguish evidence-based AI applications from unsupported claims
- Apply the VDD Framework to AI-assisted decisions
- Identify appropriate clinical use cases for AI tools
- Establish institutional governance for AI deployment

## Accessing CME Materials

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Account" icon="user">
    Sign in at [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login) using your credentials or SSO.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Education" icon="book-open">
    Access the Clinical Education section from your dashboard or navigate to `/#education`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Course" icon="play-circle">
    Choose a CME course and review its learning objectives before starting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete and Assess" icon="check-circle">
    Finish the course content and pass the assessment to earn credit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For step-by-step instructions on accessing presentations, see the [Access CME Presentations](/help-center/guides/access-cme) guide.
