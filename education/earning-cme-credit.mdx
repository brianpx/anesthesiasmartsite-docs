## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite offers accredited CME activities that allow you to earn continuing education credits while learning about AI integration in anesthesiology and evidence-based perioperative practices.

## Requirements for CME Credit

To earn CME credit on the platform, you must:

1. **Complete the educational activity** — Review all required content (presentation, reading materials, or interactive modules)
2. **Pass the post-activity assessment** — Achieve the minimum passing score on the associated quiz or evaluation
3. **Complete the evaluation form** — Provide feedback on the educational activity (required for accreditation compliance)

## Step-by-Step Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a CME Activity" icon="book-open">
    Navigate to a CME-eligible activity from the [CME Materials](/education/cme-materials) page or the Education section of your dashboard. CME-eligible activities are clearly labeled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Content" icon="monitor">
    Review all presentation content or complete all required modules. Your progress is tracked automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pass the Assessment" icon="check-circle">
    Complete the post-activity assessment. A minimum passing score is required. You may retake the assessment if you do not pass on the first attempt — your highest score is recorded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the Evaluation" icon="edit">
    Complete the activity evaluation form. This is required for credit issuance per accreditation standards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your Certificate" icon="award">
    Upon successful completion, your CME certificate is available immediately from the completions section of your profile. Download it for your records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reporting Credits

After earning CME credit:

- **Download your certificate** from your profile's completions section
- **Report to your state medical board** per your state's CME reporting requirements
- **Report to specialty boards** (e.g., ABA MOCA) as applicable to your certification maintenance program
- **Retain documentation** for the required record-keeping period (varies by state and board)

<Callout kind="info">
  Anesthesia SmartSite provides the CME certificate and credit documentation. Reporting credits to your medical board or specialty society is your responsibility. Check with your board for specific reporting deadlines and procedures.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

- **Assessment score not recorded:** See [Quiz Not Saving Progress](/help-center/troubleshooting/quiz-progress)
- **Certificate not available:** Confirm all three requirements are met (content, assessment, evaluation). If all are complete and the certificate is still unavailable, contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com)
