## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite delivers evidence-based resources tailored for anesthesiologists. You access AI-driven tools, clinical education modules, and protocols to enhance patient safety and clinical decision-making. Key features include the Verify-Decide-Document (`VDD`) framework for AI use, interactive quizzes, and comprehensive neuroanesthesia guidelines.

<Callout kind="info">
  All content follows evidence-based standards, ensuring 100% reliable guidance for perioperative care.
</Callout>
