---

## Quiz Progress Not Saving

If your quiz answers or progress are not being saved:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Internet Connection" icon="wifi">
    Quiz progress requires an active internet connection. Verify your connection is stable before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm You Are Logged In" icon="user">
    Progress is saved to your account. If your session has expired, log in again before restarting the quiz. Check for a login prompt or session timeout message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable Browser Extensions" icon="sliders">
    Ad blockers, privacy extensions, or script blockers can interfere with quiz functionality. Try disabling extensions or use an incognito/private browsing window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache" icon="trash">
    Stale cached data can cause issues. Clear your browser cache and cookies for the Anesthesia SmartSite domain, then reload the quiz page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a Different Browser" icon="globe">
    If the issue persists, try a different supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Score Not Appearing After Submission

If you submitted a quiz but your score is not showing:

1. Wait a few moments and refresh the page — scoring may take a brief time to process
2. Check the **Completions** section of your profile for recorded scores
3. If the score still does not appear, retake the quiz — your highest score is retained

## CME Assessment Score Not Linking to Credit

If you passed a CME-linked assessment but the credit is not appearing:

1. Confirm you also completed the **evaluation form** (required for credit issuance)
2. Check the **Completions** section of your profile — certificates may take a few minutes to generate
3. If all requirements are met and the certificate is still unavailable, contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com)

## Still Having Issues?

Contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with:
- The quiz name and URL
- Your browser and device information
- A description of the issue (progress not saving, score not appearing, etc.)
