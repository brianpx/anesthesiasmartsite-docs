---

## Symptoms

- The page displays a blank screen or loading spinner that does not resolve
- An error message appears (e.g., "Page could not be displayed," "Connection timed out")
- Content loads partially but some elements are missing

## Troubleshooting Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache" icon="trash">
    Cached data can cause loading issues. Clear your browser's cache and cookies for the Anesthesia SmartSite domain, then reload the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Internet Connection" icon="wifi">
    Verify your connection is active and stable. Try loading another website to confirm general internet access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable Browser Extensions" icon="sliders">
    Ad blockers, privacy extensions, and content filters can interfere with page loading. Try disabling extensions or loading the page in an incognito/private browsing window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Institutional Firewall" icon="shield">
    Hospital and institutional networks may block certain web resources. If you are on an institutional network:

    - Try accessing the site from a personal network or mobile data
    - Contact your IT department to verify that anesthesiasmartsite.com and its subdomains are not blocked
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a Different Browser" icon="globe">
    Test with a different supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). This helps determine if the issue is browser-specific.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a Different Device" icon="smartphone">
    If the issue persists across browsers, try accessing the platform from a different device (mobile phone, tablet, or another computer).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Causes

| Cause | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Stale browser cache | Clear cache and cookies, reload |
| Ad blocker interference | Disable ad blocker or add site to allowlist |
| Institutional firewall | Contact IT to allowlist the domain |
| Outdated browser | Update to the latest version |
| Network interruption | Check connection, try different network |

## Still Not Working?

Contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with:
- Your browser name and version
- Your device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Whether you are on an institutional or personal network
- Any error messages displayed (screenshot if possible)
