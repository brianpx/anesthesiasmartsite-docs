---
title: Anesthesia SmartSite won't load
description: Quick fix for loading errors on Anesthesia SmartSite.
---

## Symptoms

- Homepage does not load
- Error: "Page could not be displayed"

## Causes

- Browser cache issue
- Network connection interruption

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    In your browser settings, clear cached files and cookies. Close and reopen your browser.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````bash
    ping anesthesiasmartsite.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different device">
    Log in from a mobile phone or another computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
