## Password Reset Not Working

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email" icon="mail">
    The reset email may take a few minutes. Check your spam or junk folder. Search for emails from Anesthesia SmartSite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Email Address" icon="check-circle">
    Confirm you are entering the same email address you registered with. If you used SSO, your account may be linked to your institutional email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Again" icon="refresh-cw">
    Request a new reset link. Previous links expire after a limited time. Use the most recent link only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support" icon="mail">
    If the reset email still does not arrive, contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with your registered email address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## SSO Login Errors

If SSO login fails:

1. **Confirm your institution supports SSO** — Check with your IT department that Anesthesia SmartSite SSO is configured
2. **Try a different browser** — Some institutional SSO configurations have browser-specific issues
3. **Clear cookies** — Stale SSO session cookies can cause authentication loops
4. **Check institutional network** — Some SSO providers require you to be on the institutional network or VPN

For institutional SSO setup, see [SSO Configuration](/help-center/troubleshooting/sso-configuration).

## 2FA Lockout

If you cannot access your authenticator app:

1. Use one of your **recovery codes** (saved during 2FA setup) to log in
2. After logging in with a recovery code, disable and re-enable 2FA with your current authenticator
3. If you have no recovery codes available, contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) for account recovery (identity verification required)

<Callout kind="warning">
  Account recovery without 2FA codes requires identity verification and may take 24-48 hours. Store your recovery codes in a secure location to avoid this process.
</Callout>

## Account Locked

Accounts may be temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or reset your password. If the lock persists, contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com).

## Still Having Issues?

Contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with:
- Your registered email address
- The error message you see (screenshot if possible)
- The login method you are using (email/password or SSO)
- Your browser and device information
