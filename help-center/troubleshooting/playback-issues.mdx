## Presentation Not Loading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Internet Connection" icon="wifi">
    Presentations require a stable internet connection for streaming. Verify your connection speed is sufficient for media playback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the Page" icon="refresh-cw">
    A simple page refresh often resolves loading issues. If the presentation was partially loaded, refreshing will restart the loading process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable Ad Blockers" icon="sliders">
    Ad blockers and content filtering extensions can block media resources. Temporarily disable them or add Anesthesia SmartSite to your allowlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Institutional Firewall" icon="shield">
    Hospital and institutional networks may block streaming media or specific content delivery domains. If you are on an institutional network, try accessing the presentation from a personal network or contact your IT department to allowlist the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a Different Browser" icon="globe">
    If the issue persists, try a different supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Audio Issues

- **No audio:** Check your device volume and ensure the presentation is not muted. Verify your browser has permission to play audio.
- **Audio out of sync:** Refresh the page. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache and reload.

## Presentation Progress Not Saving

If your presentation progress is not being tracked, ensure you are logged in and your session has not expired. See [Quiz Not Saving Progress](/help-center/troubleshooting/quiz-progress) for additional troubleshooting steps that also apply to presentation progress tracking.

## Still Having Issues?

Contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with:
- The presentation name
- Your browser and device information
- A description of the issue (not loading, buffering, audio problems, etc.)
- Whether you are on an institutional or personal network
