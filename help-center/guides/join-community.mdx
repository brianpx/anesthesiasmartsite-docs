## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Community Forum" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [community.anesthesiasmartsite.com](https://community.anesthesiasmartsite.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">
    Register using your Anesthesia SmartSite credentials or create a separate community account. Use your professional identity for credible interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Introduce Yourself" icon="message-circle">
    Post in the Welcome thread to introduce yourself to the community.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Discussion Topics" icon="compass">
    Browse discussions by topic: AI in Practice, Clinical Protocols, Education & CME, Platform Feedback, or General Discussion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="warning">
  Never post patient-identifiable information. All case discussions must be fully de-identified per HIPAA and institutional privacy policies.
</Callout>

For community guidelines and discussion areas, see the full [Community Forum](/community/forum) guide.
