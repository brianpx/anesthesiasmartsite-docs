## How to Complete a Quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Quiz" icon="play-circle">
    Access quizzes from the **AI Resources** section of your dashboard, or navigate directly from the [Knowledge Quizzes](/ai/quizzes) documentation page. Select the quiz you want to take.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer All Questions" icon="edit">
    Read each question carefully and select your answer. Scenario-based questions present clinical situations where you apply the VDD framework or AI governance principles. Answer all questions before submitting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Responses" icon="check-circle">
    Select **Submit** to complete the quiz. Your answers are scored immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Results" icon="bar-chart">
    After submission, review your score and the detailed explanations for each question. Incorrect answers include references to the relevant documentation for further study.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retake If Needed" icon="refresh-cw">
    You can retake quizzes to improve your understanding. For CME-linked assessments, your highest score is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Review the relevant documentation before taking a quiz. The [VDD Framework](/ai/vdd-framework) and [AI Governance](/ai/governance) guides cover the material assessed in most quizzes.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

If your quiz progress is not saving, see [Quiz Not Saving Progress](/help-center/troubleshooting/quiz-progress).
