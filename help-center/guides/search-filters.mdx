## Using Search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Search Bar" icon="search">
    Click the search bar at the top of any page to open the search interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Keywords" icon="type">
    Type keywords related to the content you need — drug names, procedure types, clinical topics, or abbreviations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Select Results" icon="list">
    Results appear grouped by section. Select the most relevant result to navigate to that page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Applying Content Filters

After searching, narrow results using filters:

- **Section** — AI Resources, Clinical Protocols, Education, Help Center
- **Content type** — Protocols, Guidelines, CME Materials, FAQs
- **Topic** — Neuroanesthesia, Perioperative Management, AI Governance

Combine multiple filters for precise results.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use specific clinical terminology for better results. Search for "dexmedetomidine loading dose" rather than "sedation drug amount."
</Callout>

For a complete guide, see [Search & Filtering](/account/search).
