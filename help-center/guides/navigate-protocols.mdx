## Finding Protocols

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Protocols Section" icon="clipboard-list">
    From your dashboard, select **Protocols** or navigate to `/#protocols`. You can also access protocols from the Documentation sidebar under **Clinical Protocols**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse by Topic" icon="list">
    Protocols are organized by clinical area:

    - **Awake Spine Surgery** — Full procedural protocol
    - **Patient Selection** — Criteria for candidate assessment
    - **Anesthetic Techniques** — Drug protocols and sedation scoring
    - **Perioperative Management** — General checklists and criteria
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Specific Content" icon="search">
    Use the platform search bar to find specific protocol content by keyword (e.g., "dexmedetomidine," "Ramsay score," "MAC").
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Protocol Structure

Each protocol page follows a consistent structure:

1. **Overview** — What the protocol covers and when it applies
2. **Clinical content** — Step-by-step procedures, criteria tables, and drug dosing
3. **Clinical disclaimer** — Reminder to adapt to institutional policies
4. **Related protocols** — Links to related content

<Callout kind="tip">
  Save frequently referenced protocols to your [Favorites](/account/favorites) for quick access during cases.
</Callout>
