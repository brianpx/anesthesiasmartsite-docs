## How to Access CME Presentations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Account" icon="user">
    Sign in at [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Clinical Education" icon="book-open">
    From your dashboard, select **Clinical Education** or navigate to `/#education`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a CME Activity" icon="monitor">
    Choose a CME-eligible presentation from the list. Each listing shows the topic, estimated duration, and credit information. The **Beyond the Hype** presentation is the current flagship CME course.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Content" icon="play-circle">
    Review all presentation slides and materials. Your progress is saved automatically — you can pause and resume at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pass the Assessment" icon="check-circle">
    After completing the presentation, take the post-activity assessment. A minimum passing score is required for CME credit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your Certificate" icon="award">
    Upon passing, access your certificate from the **Completions** section of your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  For detailed information on earning and reporting CME credit, see [Earning CME Credit](/education/earning-cme-credit).
</Callout>
