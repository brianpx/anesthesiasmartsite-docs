## Enabling Dark Mode

Dark mode reduces screen brightness and uses a dark background, optimized for low-light environments common during night shifts and on-call coverage.

### Manual Toggle

1. Locate the display toggle in the top navigation bar (sun/moon icon)
2. Select the toggle to switch between light and dark mode
3. Your preference is saved and persists across sessions

### Auto-Detect (System Preference)

1. Navigate to **Profile & Preferences** in your account settings
2. Under **Display Preferences**, set the theme to **Auto**
3. The platform will follow your device's system-wide light/dark mode setting

<Callout kind="tip">
  Auto-detect mode is convenient if your device already switches to dark mode on a schedule (e.g., after sunset). The platform will match your system setting automatically.
</Callout>

## Supported Across All Content

Dark mode applies to all platform pages including:
- Documentation and protocols
- Quiz interfaces
- Dashboard and navigation
- Help Center articles
