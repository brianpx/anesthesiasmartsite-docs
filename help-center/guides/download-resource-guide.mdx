## Downloading the Resource Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to AI Resources" icon="zap">
    From your dashboard, select **AI Resources** or navigate to `/#ai`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Resource Guide" icon="book-open">
    Select **Resource Guide** to view the curated collection of articles and frameworks on clinical AI governance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the PDF" icon="download">
    Select the **Download Guide** button to save a PDF version for offline reference or institutional distribution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  The resource guide is updated regularly. Check the version date on the downloaded PDF and re-download periodically for the latest content.
</Callout>

For details about what the resource guide contains, see [Resource Guide & Articles](/ai/resource-guide).
