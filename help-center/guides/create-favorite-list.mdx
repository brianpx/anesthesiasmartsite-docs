---
title: Creating a Favorite Guidelines List
description: How to save and access quick-reference guideline lists.
---

## Overview

Save your most-used guidelines for easy access.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a guideline">
    Navigate to any guideline page you reference often.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Favorites">
    ````
    Click the ⭐️ "Add to Favorites" button at the top of the guideline.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access favorites">
    ````
    Select your "Favorites" list from the main menu. All saved guidelines appear here.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
