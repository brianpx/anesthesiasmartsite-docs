## Adding a Resource to Favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a Resource" icon="file-text">
    Open any guideline, protocol, or educational resource you want to save for quick access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Favorites" icon="star">
    Select the **Add to Favorites** button (star icon) at the top of the page. The icon fills in to confirm the item has been saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Favorites" icon="list">
    View all saved items from the **Favorites** section in your profile or through the favorites shortcut in the dashboard navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your List

- **Remove an item:** Navigate to the resource and deselect the star icon, or select the remove option from your favorites list
- **Browse by type:** Your favorites list shows the resource type (protocol, guideline, CME material) alongside each item

<Callout kind="tip">
  Keep your favorites list focused on the resources you reference most during clinical work. A short, curated list is faster to navigate than a long one.
</Callout>

For the full guide on favorites and bookmarks, see [Favorites & Bookmarks](/account/favorites).
