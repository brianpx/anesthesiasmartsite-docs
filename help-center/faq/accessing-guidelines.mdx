## Overview

You can browse or search for evidence-based anesthesia guidelines quickly.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://anesthesiasmartsite.com`
2. Log in with your hospital or email credentials
3. Click the "Guidelines" tab in the main menu
4. Search or filter by specialty, procedure, or topic
5. Tap any guideline to view full details

<Callout kind="info">
All guidelines are regularly reviewed and updated for accuracy.
</Callout>
