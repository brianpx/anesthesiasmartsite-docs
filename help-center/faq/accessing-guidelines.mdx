## Finding Guidelines and Protocols

Clinical guidelines and protocols are accessible from multiple entry points on the platform.

### From the Dashboard

1. Log in at [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login)
2. Select **Protocols** from the dashboard to browse all available clinical protocols
3. Use the section navigation to find specific protocol categories (Awake Spine Surgery, Patient Selection, Anesthetic Techniques, Perioperative Management)

### Using Search

1. Open the search bar at the top of any page
2. Enter keywords related to the guideline you need (e.g., "dexmedetomidine," "patient selection," "MAC")
3. Filter results by **Clinical Protocols** to narrow to guideline content

### From the Documentation

Browse the [Protocols Overview](/protocols/overview) page for a complete index of available clinical protocols with descriptions.

## Types of Guidelines Available

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Procedural Protocols** | Step-by-step procedural workflows | [Awake Spine Surgery](/protocols/awake-spine-surgery) |
| **Selection Criteria** | Structured patient assessment criteria | [Patient Selection](/protocols/patient-selection) |
| **Technique Guides** | Drug dosing, monitoring, and technique details | [Anesthetic Techniques](/protocols/anesthetic-techniques) |
| **Management Checklists** | Pre-op, intra-op, and post-op checklists | [Perioperative Management](/protocols/perioperative-management) |

## Saving Guidelines for Quick Access

Save frequently referenced guidelines to your [Favorites](/account/favorites) for quick retrieval during clinical work.

  All clinical content is reviewed against published guidelines and current evidence. Protocols are updated when new evidence or guideline changes warrant revision — check the [Changelog](/changelog) for recent updates.
</Callout>
