---
title: CME Credit & Certificates FAQ
description: Common questions about earning CME credit, accessing certificates, reporting credits, and accreditation on Anesthesia SmartSite.
---

## What CME credit is available on the platform?

Anesthesia SmartSite offers AMA PRA Category 1 Credit(s) through accredited educational activities. See [CME Materials](/education/cme-materials) for the current list of available CME courses.

## How do I earn CME credit?

Complete three steps: (1) finish the educational content, (2) pass the post-activity assessment, and (3) submit the evaluation form. See [Earning CME Credit](/education/earning-cme-credit) for detailed instructions.

## Where do I find my CME certificate?

After completing all requirements for a CME activity, your certificate is available in the **Completions** section of your profile. Download it for your records.

## How do I report credits to my medical board?

Download your certificate from the platform and submit it to your state medical board or specialty board per their reporting requirements. Anesthesia SmartSite provides the documentation; reporting is your responsibility.

## Can I retake a CME assessment?

Yes. If you do not pass the post-activity assessment on the first attempt, you may retake it. Your highest score is recorded.

## My assessment score is not saving. What should I do?

See [Quiz Not Saving Progress](/help-center/troubleshooting/quiz-progress) for troubleshooting steps. If the issue persists, contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com).

## How long are CME certificates valid?

Certificates reflect the date of completion and do not expire. However, your medical board or specialty society may have specific timeframes for when credits must be earned. Check with your board for requirements.

## Is there a cost for CME activities?

Access to CME activities is included with your Anesthesia SmartSite account. Check individual course listings for any specific eligibility requirements.
