---

## How do I create an account?

Visit [anesthesiasmartsite.com/signup](https://anesthesiasmartsite.com/signup), enter your name, email, and a secure password, then verify your email through the confirmation link. See the full [Account Setup](/account/setup) guide for details.

## How do I log in?

Go to [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login) and sign in with your email/password or your institution's SSO provider.

## How do I reset my password?

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Select **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Follow the reset link sent to your email
5. Create a new password meeting the minimum requirements (12 characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols)

If the reset email does not arrive, check your spam folder or contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com).

## How do I change my email address?

Update your email from **Profile Settings** in your account menu. You will need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

## How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Navigate to **Security Settings** in your profile, select **Enable 2FA**, and follow the setup steps. See [Security & 2FA](/account/security) for a detailed walkthrough.

## Can I use SSO to log in?

Yes. If your institution supports SSO through Google Workspace or SAML, select **Login with SSO** on the login page and choose your provider. For SSO setup issues, see [SSO Configuration](/help-center/troubleshooting/sso-configuration).

## How do I delete my account?

Contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) to request account deletion. Include your registered email address in the request. Account deletion is permanent and removes all associated data including CME completion records.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Download any CME certificates before requesting account deletion. Once deleted, completion records cannot be recovered.
</Callout>
