## What data does Anesthesia SmartSite collect?

The platform collects account information (name, email, institution), usage data (pages viewed, quizzes completed, CME progress), and optional professional details you choose to provide (license number, specialty).

## Does the platform store patient data?

No. Anesthesia SmartSite is an educational platform. It does not collect, store, or process patient health information (PHI). Clinical protocols and tools are for reference; patient data remains in your institutional systems.

## Is the platform HIPAA compliant?

Anesthesia SmartSite does not handle PHI and is not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA. If your institution integrates AI tools referenced on the platform into clinical workflows, those integrations are governed by your institution's HIPAA compliance program.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Never enter patient-identifiable information into the platform, community forum, quizzes, or any feedback forms. Use de-identified information only when discussing clinical scenarios.
</Callout>

## How is my account data protected?

- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available and recommended
- Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Session management includes configurable timeouts and device tracking

See [Security & 2FA](/account/security) for account protection details.

## Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes. Contact [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) to request account and data deletion. This action is permanent. Download CME certificates before requesting deletion.

## Who has access to my usage data?

Usage data is used to improve platform content and personalize recommendations. It is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Aggregate, de-identified usage statistics may be used for platform improvement analytics.

## How do I report a security concern?

Report security concerns or suspected vulnerabilities to [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) with the subject line "Security Concern."
