## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite supports multiple layers of account security to protect your professional data and activity. Two-factor authentication (2FA) is strongly recommended for all accounts.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

2FA adds a second verification step to your login, requiring both your password and a time-based code from an authenticator app.

### Setting Up 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Security Settings" icon="settings">
    Navigate to your profile and select **Security Settings** or access it from the account menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA" icon="lock">
    Select **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**. A QR code will be displayed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR Code" icon="smartphone">
    Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator, or similar) and scan the QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Verification Code" icon="check-circle">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app to confirm setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Recovery Codes" icon="download">
    Download or record your recovery codes in a secure location. These codes allow account access if you lose your authenticator device.

    <Callout kind="warning">
      Store recovery codes securely and separately from your primary device. If you lose both your authenticator and recovery codes, you will need to contact support for account recovery.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Requirements

- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols
- Cannot reuse your last 5 passwords
- Change your password from **Security Settings** in your profile

### Resetting Your Password

1. Navigate to the login page at [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login)
2. Select **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Follow the reset link in your email to create a new password

For password reset issues, see [Login & Authentication Issues](/help-center/troubleshooting/login-issues).

## Session Management

- Sessions expire after a configurable period of inactivity
- Active sessions are visible in your Security Settings
- You can terminate individual sessions or sign out of all devices
- SSO sessions follow your institution's session policies

## Security Best Practices

| Practice | Recommendation |
|----------|---------------|
| 2FA | Enable for all accounts, especially those with CME credit data |
| Password | Use a unique password not shared with other services |
| Shared devices | Always log out when using shared or public computers |
| Recovery codes | Store in a password manager or secure physical location |
| Suspicious activity | Report unrecognized sessions to [support@anesthesiasmartsite.com](mailto:support@anesthesiasmartsite.com) |
