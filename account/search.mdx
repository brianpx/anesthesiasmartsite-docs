## Overview

The platform search allows you to find content across all sections — AI resources, clinical protocols, education materials, and help articles. Content filters let you narrow results by section, specialty, and topic.

## Using Search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Search Bar" icon="search">
    Access search from the search bar at the top of any page. The search interface appears as an overlay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Keywords" icon="type">
    Type keywords related to the content you need. Search supports:

    - **Drug names** — e.g., "dexmedetomidine," "lidocaine"
    - **Procedure types** — e.g., "awake spine," "MAC"
    - **Topics** — e.g., "VDD framework," "patient selection"
    - **Abbreviations** — e.g., "ASA," "LAST," "PONV"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results" icon="list">
    Results are grouped by section (Documentation, Help Center, Changelog) with the most relevant matches shown first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Content Filters

Narrow search results or browse content by applying filters:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Section** | AI Resources, Clinical Protocols, Education, Help Center |
| **Content type** | Protocols, Guidelines, CME Materials, Presentations, Quizzes, FAQs |
| **Topic** | Neuroanesthesia, Perioperative Management, AI Governance, VDD Framework |

Filters can be combined. For example, filter by **Section: Clinical Protocols** and **Topic: Neuroanesthesia** to see only neuroanesthesia-related protocol pages.

## Search Tips

<Callout kind="tip">
  - Use specific clinical terminology for better results (e.g., "Ramsay sedation score" instead of "sedation levels")
  - Search for drug generic names rather than brand names
  - Combine a procedure name with a phase for targeted results (e.g., "awake spine emergence")
</Callout>
