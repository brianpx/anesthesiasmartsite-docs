## Overview

Setting up your Anesthesia SmartSite account takes approximately 2 minutes. After registration, you access AI resources, clinical protocols, CME materials, and personalized recommendations.

## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [anesthesiasmartsite.com/signup](https://anesthesiasmartsite.com/signup).

    Enter your professional details:
    - Full name
    - Email address (professional email recommended)
    - Password (minimum 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Click **Create Account** to submit your registration.

    Check your email inbox for a verification link from Anesthesia SmartSite. Click the link to activate your account.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      If the verification email does not appear within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder. For persistent issues, see [Login & Authentication Issues](/help-center/troubleshooting/login-issues).
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile" icon="user">
    After verification, log in and complete your profile with optional professional details:

    - Medical license number
    - Specialty focus (e.g., neuroanesthesia, cardiac, pediatric)
    - Institution or practice name

    These details personalize your dashboard recommendations and content filtering.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In

Access your account at [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password" icon="mail">
    Enter your registered email and password, then select **Sign In**.

    After your first login, you will be prompted to enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced security. See [Security & 2FA](/account/security) for setup instructions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Single Sign-On (SSO)" icon="shield">
    If your institution supports SSO (Google Workspace, hospital SAML):

    1. Select **Login with SSO** on the login page
    2. Choose your identity provider
    3. Authorize access through your institutional login

    SSO is recommended for clinical teams with centralized identity management. For SSO setup issues, see [SSO Configuration](/help-center/troubleshooting/sso-configuration).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
