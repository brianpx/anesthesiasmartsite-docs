## Overview

The favorites system lets you bookmark guidelines, protocols, and educational resources for quick access. Build a personalized collection of the content you reference most frequently in your practice.

## Adding Favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a Resource" icon="file-text">
    Open any guideline, protocol, or educational resource on the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Favorites" icon="star">
    Select the **Add to Favorites** button (star icon) at the top of the page. The icon fills in to confirm the resource has been saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Favorites" icon="list">
    View all saved items from the **Favorites** section in your profile or through the favorites shortcut in the dashboard navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Favorites

### Viewing Favorites

Your favorites list displays all saved resources with:
- Resource title and type (protocol, guideline, CME material)
- Date added
- Link to the original resource

### Removing Favorites

To remove a resource from your favorites:
1. Navigate to the resource page and deselect the star icon, or
2. Open your favorites list and select the remove option next to the item

### Organizing Favorites

Browse your favorites list by resource type or use the search function within favorites to find specific items.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Build a focused favorites list for your most commonly referenced protocols. During cases, a short favorites list is faster to navigate than searching the full platform.
</Callout>
