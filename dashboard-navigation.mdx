## Overview

After logging in at [app.anesthesiasmartsite.com](https://app.anesthesiasmartsite.com/login), you arrive at the dashboard — your central hub for accessing AI resources, clinical education, protocols, and personalized recommendations.

## Main Sections

The dashboard organizes content into four primary sections accessible from the top navigation.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AI Resources" icon="zap">
    Access the VDD framework, AI resource guide, governance principles, and knowledge quizzes.

    Navigate here from the dashboard by selecting **AI Resources** or visiting `/#ai`.

    Key actions:
    - Review the VDD framework for supervised AI decision-making
    - Download the curated resource guide
    - Take knowledge quizzes to assess AI governance understanding
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Clinical Education" icon="book-open">
    Browse CME presentations, educational materials, and interactive learning tools.

    Navigate here from the dashboard by selecting **Clinical Education** or visiting `/#education`.

    Key actions:
    - Access the "Beyond the Hype" CME presentation
    - Review learning objectives and complete assessments
    - Track CME credit progress
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Protocols" icon="file-text">
    View evidence-based clinical protocols for neuroanesthesia and perioperative management.

    Navigate here from the dashboard by selecting **Protocols** or visiting `/#protocols`.

    Key actions:
    - Review awake spine surgery protocols
    - Check patient selection criteria
    - Access anesthetic technique guidelines
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Your Profile" icon="user">
    Manage your account settings, security preferences, and notification options.

    Access your profile from the user icon in the top-right corner.

    Key actions:
    - Update specialty focus and license information
    - Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
    - Configure notification and display preferences
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Using Search

The platform search allows you to find content across all sections. Access it from the search bar at the top of any page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search" icon="search">
    Click the search bar or use the keyboard shortcut to open the search interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Keywords" icon="type">
    Type keywords related to the content you need — drug names, procedure types, or topic areas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Results" icon="filter">
    Use the content filters to narrow results by section (AI Resources, Protocols, Education) or by specialty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Favorites

Save frequently accessed guidelines and resources for quick retrieval.

1. Navigate to any guideline, protocol, or resource page.
2. Select the **Add to Favorites** button (star icon) at the top of the page.
3. Access your saved items from the **Favorites** section of your profile.

For detailed instructions, see the [Favorites & Bookmarks](/account/favorites) guide.

## Display Settings

**Dark Mode** is available for low-light environments common during night shifts and call coverage. Toggle dark mode from your profile settings or the display toggle in the top navigation.
