## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite provides interactive knowledge quizzes to assess and reinforce your understanding of AI supervision, the VDD framework, and clinical AI governance. Quizzes are designed as both learning tools and assessments for CME credit eligibility.

## Available Quizzes

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="AI Supervision Quiz" icon="cpu">
    Covers the fundamentals of supervised AI use in clinical settings, including verification methods, appropriate use cases, and limitations of AI in perioperative care.

    **Format:** Multiple choice and scenario-based questions
    **Access:** [AI Resources section](https://anesthesiasmartsite.com/ai/quiz.html) on your dashboard
  </Card>
  <Card title="VDD Framework Quiz" icon="shield">
    Assesses your understanding of the Verify-Decide-Document methodology through clinical scenarios requiring you to apply each step of the framework.

    **Format:** Case-based scenarios with structured responses
    **Access:** [VDD Quiz section](https://anesthesiasmartsite.com/quizzes/ai-vdd.html) on your dashboard
  </Card>
  <Card title="Beyond the Hype Assessment" icon="monitor">
    Post-presentation assessment for the Beyond the Hype CME. Completion with a passing score is required for CME credit.

    **Format:** Post-presentation assessment questions
    **Access:** Available after completing the [Beyond the Hype presentation](/ai/beyond-the-hype)
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Taking a Quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Quiz" icon="play-circle">
    Navigate to the quiz from the AI Resources section of your dashboard or use the direct links above.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete All Questions" icon="edit">
    Answer each question to the best of your knowledge. Scenario-based questions present clinical situations where you apply the VDD framework or AI governance principles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Results" icon="bar-chart">
    After submission, review your score and the explanations for each answer. Incorrect answers include references to the relevant documentation for further study.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retake If Needed" icon="refresh-cw">
    You can retake quizzes to improve your score. For CME-linked assessments, your highest score is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quiz and CME Credit

Certain quizzes are linked to CME credit eligibility:

| Quiz | CME Linked | Minimum Passing Score |
|------|------------|----------------------|
| AI Supervision Quiz | No | N/A (self-assessment) |
| VDD Framework Quiz | No | N/A (self-assessment) |
| Beyond the Hype Assessment | Yes | Required for credit |

For quizzes linked to CME credit, see [Earning CME Credit](/education/earning-cme-credit) for details on credit reporting and certificate access.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Self-assessment quizzes (AI Supervision and VDD) have no minimum score requirement. Use them as learning tools — review the explanations for any questions you find challenging, then revisit the relevant documentation.
</Callout>
