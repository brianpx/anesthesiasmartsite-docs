## Overview

The AI Resource Guide is a curated collection of articles, frameworks, and references for clinicians seeking to understand AI's role in anesthesiology. Each resource is selected for clinical relevance and alignment with evidence-based practice.

<Callout kind="info">
  The resource guide is updated regularly as new evidence and guidelines are published. Check back for new additions or [subscribe to updates](https://anesthesiasmartsite.com/subscribe) for notifications.
</Callout>

## Accessing the Resource Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to AI Resources" icon="zap">
    From your dashboard, select **AI Resources** or navigate to `/#ai`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Resource Guide" icon="book-open">
    Select **Resource Guide** to view the full curated collection. You can browse by category or search for specific topics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download for Offline Use" icon="download">
    Select **Download Guide** to save a PDF version for offline reference or institutional distribution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resource Categories

The guide organizes resources into the following categories:

### Clinical AI Governance

Articles and frameworks addressing institutional oversight, policy development, and regulatory considerations for AI in healthcare settings.

- Establishing AI review committees in anesthesiology departments
- Regulatory landscape for AI-assisted clinical tools
- Liability considerations for AI-informed clinical decisions

### Implementation Strategies

Practical guidance for integrating AI tools into existing clinical workflows without disrupting patient care.

- Pilot program design for new AI tools
- Change management strategies for clinical AI adoption
- Measuring ROI and clinical impact of AI deployments

### Evidence-Based AI Practices

Published research and guidelines on AI applications specifically in anesthesiology and perioperative medicine.

- AI in hemodynamic monitoring and prediction
- Machine learning for pre-operative risk stratification
- Natural language processing in anesthesia documentation

### The VDD Framework

Resources specifically related to the Verify-Decide-Document methodology.

- VDD framework development and validation
- Case studies of VDD application in clinical settings
- Training materials for institutional VDD rollout

## Using the Resource Guide in Your Institution

The resource guide is designed for both individual learning and institutional education programs:

- **Journal clubs** — Select articles for monthly case-based discussions
- **Grand rounds** — Use governance frameworks as the basis for department presentations
- **Onboarding** — Include the VDD-focused resources in new hire training packages
- **Policy development** — Reference governance articles when drafting institutional AI policies
