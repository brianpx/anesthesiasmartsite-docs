## AI in Anesthesiology

Anesthesia SmartSite takes a clinician-first approach to artificial intelligence in perioperative care. AI supports your clinical decision-making but never replaces it. Every AI tool and framework on this platform is designed around a core principle: **supervised AI deployment with structured verification**.

<Callout kind="info">
  AI supports clinical decisions but never replaces your judgment. Every recommendation must be verified against evidence and patient-specific data before action.
</Callout>

## Core Framework: Verify, Decide, Document

The **VDD Framework** is the foundation of responsible AI use on this platform. It provides a structured, repeatable process for every AI-assisted clinical decision:

1. **Verify** — Cross-check AI outputs against evidence-based protocols and patient data
2. **Decide** — Weigh AI input alongside your clinical experience and choose the safest course
3. **Document** — Record AI use, verification steps, and clinical rationale in the patient chart
