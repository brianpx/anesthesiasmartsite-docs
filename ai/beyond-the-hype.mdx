## Overview

**Beyond the Hype** is an accredited continuing medical education (CME) presentation examining the responsible deployment of artificial intelligence in anesthesiology. Developed in collaboration with the [International Anesthesia Research Society (IARS)](https://iars.org), this presentation moves past AI marketing claims to focus on practical, evidence-based integration of AI tools in perioperative care.

<Callout kind="info">
  This CME presentation was featured at the IARS 2025 meeting. Access it through the AI Resources section of your dashboard.
</Callout>

## Learning Objectives

After completing this presentation, you will be able to:

1. Distinguish between evidence-based AI applications and unsupported claims in anesthesiology
2. Apply the VDD Framework (Verify, Decide, Document) to AI-assisted clinical decisions
3. Identify appropriate use cases for AI tools in perioperative care
4. Recognize the limitations and risks of AI in clinical settings
5. Establish governance structures for AI tool evaluation and deployment at your institution

## Accessing the Presentation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to AI Resources" icon="zap">
    From your dashboard, select **AI Resources** or navigate to `/#ai`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Beyond the Hype" icon="monitor">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Assessment" icon="check-circle">
    After reviewing all presentation content, complete the post-presentation assessment to earn CME credit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Certificate" icon="award">
    Upon passing the assessment, download your CME certificate from the completions section of your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Presentation Topics

The presentation covers the following areas:

| Topic | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| AI in Clinical Context | Current state of AI in anesthesiology vs. marketing claims |
| Evidence Assessment | How to evaluate AI tool claims against published evidence |
| VDD Framework | Structured methodology for supervised AI use |
| Clinical Scenarios | Real-world examples of appropriate and inappropriate AI use |
| Institutional Governance | Building review processes for AI tool adoption |
| Patient Safety | Maintaining safety standards when integrating AI |

## CME Credit Information

- **Credit type:** AMA PRA Category 1 Credit(s)
- **Prerequisites:** Active Anesthesia SmartSite account
- **Assessment:** Post-presentation quiz (minimum passing score required)
- **Certificate:** Available immediately upon passing

For detailed information on earning and reporting CME credit, see [Earning CME Credit](/education/earning-cme-credit).
