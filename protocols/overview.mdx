## Evidence Review Process

All protocols on Anesthesia SmartSite follow a structured review process:

1. **Literature review** — Protocols are developed from published guidelines, systematic reviews, and society recommendations (ASA, SNACC, SPA)

2. **Clinical validation** — Content is reviewed by practicing anesthesiologists for clinical accuracy and applicability

3. **Regular updates** — Protocols are revised when new evidence, guideline updates, or safety alerts warrant changes

4. **Version tracking** — All protocol changes are documented in the [changelog](/changelog) with the evidence basis for changes
